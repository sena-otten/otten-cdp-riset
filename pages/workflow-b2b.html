📈 Customer Health Score Definition

Health Score adalah metrik komposit (0-100) yang mengukur kesehatan hubungan dengan customer B2B. Score ini digunakan untuk prioritas retention dan prediksi churn.

Health Score Components

Factor Weight Score 100 Score 70 Score 40 Score 0
Order Recency 25% 0-30 hari 31-60 hari 61-90 hari >90 hari
Order Frequency 20% >100% vs avg 75-100% 50-75% <50%
Order Value Trend 15% Growing >10% Stable 10% Declining 10-30% Declining >30%
NPS/CSAT Score 15% Promoter (9-10) Passive (7-8) Detractor (5-6) Detractor (0-4)
Support Tickets 10% 0 open tickets 1-2 tickets 3-4 tickets 5+ atau unresolved >7d
Payment Behavior 15% Always on-time 1-7 days late 8-14 days late >14 days late

💚

🤖 AI Health Score Enhancement:
