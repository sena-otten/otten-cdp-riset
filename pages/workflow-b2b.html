💬 Sales Staff Notification Channel

Setiap sales staff dapat memilih channel notifikasi yang preferred:

Channel Requirement Keterangan
Push B2B App B2B App installed + logged in Primary channel, real-time notification di mobile app
Telegram Nomor HP terdaftar + Telegram active Direct message ke personal Telegram, lebih personal
Email Email valid Fallback jika Push & Telegram tidak tersedia
⚙ Staff Channel Preference:
  • preferred_channel: push_app / telegram / email
  • telegram_phone: Nomor HP untuk Telegram (format: +62xxx)
  • fallback_enabled: true/false - kirim ke channel lain jika preferred gagal
  • Setting diatur per staff di profile masing-masing
