2. Business Requirements

2.1 Problem Statement

⚠ Current Pain Points

  • Data customer tersebar di multiple systems
  • Tidak ada visibility pipeline real-time
  • Manual follow-up & tracking
  • Inconsistent customer experience
  • No unified messaging platform

📊 Business Impact

  • Lost leads due to slow response
  • Missed upsell opportunities
  • Inefficient sales process
  • Poor customer retention
  • No accurate forecasting

2.2 Business Goals

Goal Description Target
Pipeline Visibility Real-time tracking semua deals B2B & B2C 100%
Lead Response Reduce time to first contact <24 hours
Conversion Rate Improve lead-to-customer conversion +25%
Customer Retention Reduce churn through proactive engagement +40%
Revenue Forecast Accurate B2B revenue prediction 85% accuracy

2.4 In Scope / Out of Scope

✅ In Scope

  • Customer management (B2B & B2C)
  • Sales pipeline & deal tracking
  • Activity recording & history
  • Multi-channel messaging (WA, Email, Push)
  • AI automation triggers
  • Basic reporting & dashboard
  • Integration with existing apps

❌ Out of Scope

  • E-commerce functionality
  • Inventory management
  • Accounting & invoicing
  • HR & payroll
  • Advanced BI & data warehouse
  • Mobile app development (use existing)
