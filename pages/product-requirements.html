3. Product Requirements (PRD)

3.1 Users & Personas

💼

B2B Sales Rep

Field sales untuk cafe, hotel, office

Goals
Close deals, meet quota, manage relationships
Pain Points
Manual tracking, no mobile access, lost follow-ups
Needs
Mobile CRM, automated reminders, pipeline view
📞

CS Agent

Handle inbound inquiries & support

Goals
Quick resolution, customer satisfaction
Pain Points
No customer history, switching apps, manual tickets
Needs
360 customer view, quick replies, ticket system
📊

Sales Manager

Team lead & performance oversight

Goals
Team performance, accurate forecasting
Pain Points
No real-time data, manual reports
Needs
Dashboard, pipeline reports, team activity

3.3 Functional Requirements

👥 Customer Management

  • B2B: Legal Entity - Brand - Location
  • B2C: Personal profiles
  • Segment-specific fields
  • Contact & channel preferences
  • Activity history timeline

📈 Pipeline & Deals

  • Stage management with SLA
  • Deal value & forecasting
  • Won/Lost with reason
  • Product dimension tagging
  • PIC assignment

📝 Activity Recording

  • Call, visit, meeting logging
  • Email & WhatsApp tracking
  • Transaction linking
  • Notes & attachments
  • Next action scheduling

💬 Messaging

  • WhatsApp integration
  • Email campaigns
  • Push notifications
  • Template management
  • Opt-in/out handling

3.4 Business Rules

Area Rule
Customer Group 1 customer = 1 group (B2B or B2C). Semua deal mewarisi customer_group.
B2B Entity Legal Entity (1) - Brand (N) - Location (N). Invoice ke Legal Entity.
Pipeline Won/Lost wajib reason. Retention hanya untuk Won. Setiap stage ada PIC & SLA.
Messaging Fallback: Push - WhatsApp - Email. Harus opt-in per channel.
Activity Setiap activity harus punya next_action dan next_action_date.

3.5 Non-Goals

⚠ Explicitly Not Included:
  • Full marketing automation suite
  • Complete e-commerce platform rebuild
  • Advanced predictive analytics / ML models
  • Custom mobile app (use existing B2B & Retail apps)
  • Third-party marketplace integrations (Tokopedia, Shopee)
