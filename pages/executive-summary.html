1. Executive Summary

1.1 Background & Vision

Otten Coffee saat ini memiliki multiple touchpoints (B2B App, E-commerce App, OTSAR, Customer Service) yang menghasilkan data customer tersebar di berbagai sistem. Untuk meningkatkan customer experience dan revenue, diperlukan Customer Data Platform (CDP) sebagai fondasi yang menyatukan semua data customer, dilengkapi dengan 4 Fitur Utama untuk mendukung operasional bisnis:

1. Sales
Pipeline management, Lead tracking, Visit planning, Deal & quotation
2. Customer Service
Unified inbox, Ticketing, Complaint handling, Knowledge base
3. After Sales
Machine maintenance, Service scheduling, Warranty, Spare parts
4. Marketing
Manual campaigns, Automation journeys, Broadcast, A/B testing
Vision Statement: Membangun unified customer platform yang memungkinkan Otten Coffee untuk memahami setiap customer secara 360°, memberikan personalized experience, dan mengoptimalkan customer lifetime value melalui data-driven decisions.

1.2 Business Context - B2B vs B2C

Aspect B2B (Horeka & Office) B2C (Personal/Homebrewer)
Customer Entity Legal Entity > Brand > Location Individual Customer
Sales Cycle Long (weeks-months), relationship-based Short (minutes-days), transactional
Primary Channel WhatsApp, Sales Visit, Email Push Notification, In-App, Email
Key Metrics Monthly consumption (kg), Contract value Order frequency, AOV, CLV
Automation Focus Reorder reminder, Visit scheduling Cart abandonment, Recommendations
