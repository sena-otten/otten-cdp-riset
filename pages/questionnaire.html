Manager Target Leads / Retention / Revenue - Deep Dive
🎯 LEAD TARGETS
1Berapa target jumlah leads baru per bulan untuk tim sales?
Total leads, breakdown per sales rep, per segment (Cafe/Restaurant/Hotel/Office)
2Bagaimana definisi "qualified lead" di perusahaan?
Scoring criteria: budget, authority, need, timeline (BANT) atau criteria lain
3Berapa target conversion rate dari lead ke opportunity?
Current rate: ___%, Target rate: ___%, Per segment breakdown
4Berapa target conversion rate dari opportunity ke won deal?
Current win rate: ___%, Target: ___%, Per product category/segment
5Bagaimana lead distribution/assignment ke sales reps?
Round-robin, territory-based, skill-based, capacity-based?
6Apa lead response time target (dari inquiry masuk hingga first contact)?
Current avg: ___ jam, Target: ___ jam, SLA per channel
7Bagaimana tracking lead aging dan follow-up compliance?
Max lead age tanpa activity, escalation rules
🔒 RETENTION TARGETS
8Berapa target customer retention rate (CRR) B2B?
Current CRR: ___%, Target CRR: ___%, Per segment
9Berapa acceptable churn rate per bulan/quarter?
Current churn: ___%, Max acceptable: ___%, Early warning threshold
10Bagaimana definisi "at-risk customer" yang perlu intervention?
Triggers: order frequency drop, volume drop, complaint pattern, dll
11Apa target untuk win-back churned customers?
Win-back rate target: ___%, Timeline max untuk reactivation attempt
12Berapa target Net Promoter Score (NPS) untuk B2B?
Current NPS: ___, Target NPS: ___, Measurement frequency
13Bagaimana mengukur customer health score?
Komponen: order recency, frequency, monetary, engagement, support tickets
14Apa retention KPI yang menjadi tanggung jawab sales rep vs after-sales?
Ownership matrix: siapa responsible untuk metric apa
💰 REVENUE TARGETS
15Berapa target revenue total tim B2B per bulan/quarter/tahun?
Breakdown: new customer revenue vs existing customer revenue
16Berapa target revenue per sales rep?
Individual quota, tiering (junior/senior), adjustment factors
17Berapa target average deal size per segment?
Cafe: Rp___, Restaurant: Rp___, Hotel: Rp___, Office: Rp___
18Berapa target revenue dari upselling/cross-selling ke existing customers?
% dari total revenue, product categories untuk upsell
19Bagaimana target revenue di-breakdown per product category?
Coffee beans, machines, equipment, supplies - % contribution
20Berapa target Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) per segment?
Current CLV vs target, calculation method
21Apa target recurring revenue (subscription/repeat order)?
% recurring vs one-time, MRR target
22Bagaimana commission/incentive structure terkait target?
Base vs variable, threshold, accelerators, retention bonus
